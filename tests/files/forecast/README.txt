# File Descriptions

- hmd_usa_qx.txt: year and age mortality rates coming from the Human Mortality Database.
  Located at "https://www.mortality.org/Country/Country?cntr=USA"
- simple_data_2.csv: simple rates with exposures to experiment how weights affect
  predictions.
- simple_data.csv: simple rates with year, gender, and rates. Used to test how
  adjusting rates before or after model impacts the predictions.
